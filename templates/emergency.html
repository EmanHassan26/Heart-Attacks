Emergency Contacts

In Case of Emergency

If you or someone else is experiencing heart attack symptoms, call your local emergency number immediately.

International Emergency Numbers:

  • General Emergency: 123
  • Ambulance: 123
  • Police: 122
  • Fire Department: 180

Heart Health Hotlines:

  • National Heart Institute (Cairo): +202 2363 0296
  • Egyptian Heart Society: 19057

Emergency Preparedness

Being prepared can save lives. Here's what you should do:

  • Save emergency numbers in your phone
  • Know the location of the nearest hospital with 24-hour emergency cardiac care
  • Learn CPR and basic first aid
  • If you're at risk, carry aspirin (to chew during a suspected heart attack after consulting with emergency services)
  • Inform family members about your medical conditions and medications
